jane doe ENSEMBLE

Jane Doe Ensemble are a group from New York City, USA. They came together in February 2017 in response to the then (and ongoing) political climate to provide a release and counter point. The group consist of Aaron (drums/vocals), Lauren (keys/vox) and Rachel (guitar/vox).

A six track digital release is due January 2018.
